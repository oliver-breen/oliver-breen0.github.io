Welcome to My Blog

Published on February 10, 2026

Welcome to my cybersecurity blog! I'm excited to share this space with you as I document my journey, insights, and experiences in the world of cybersecurity.

About This Blog

This blog serves as a platform where I'll be sharing:

  • Technical Tutorials: Step-by-step guides on various cybersecurity tools and techniques
  • Project Walkthroughs: Detailed explanations of my home lab setup, TryHackMe rooms, and HackTheBox challenges
  • Study Resources: Guides and tips for cybersecurity certifications, particularly CompTIA Security+
  • Industry Insights: Thoughts on current trends, best practices, and lessons learned
  • Tool Reviews: My experiences with various security tools like Splunk, Wireshark, and more

My Background

With over 9 years of experience in transportation and aviation, I've developed a strong foundation in operational awareness, risk management, and attention to detail—skills that translate seamlessly into cybersecurity.

Currently, I'm completing my Bachelor's Degree of Science in Cybersecurity from Colorado State University - Global Campus. This academic journey, combined with hands-on practice through platforms like TryHackMe and HackTheBox, has solidified my passion for information security.

Why Cybersecurity?

The decision to transition into cybersecurity came from a natural intersection of my interests:

  • Problem-Solving: I thrive on analyzing complex situations and finding solutions
  • Continuous Learning: The ever-evolving nature of cybersecurity keeps me intellectually engaged
  • Real-World Impact: Protecting systems and data has tangible benefits for organizations and individuals
  • Analytical Mindset: My experience in risk identification naturally aligns with threat analysis

What to Expect

In the coming weeks and months, I'll be publishing posts covering various topics including:

  • Setting up and maintaining a cybersecurity home lab
  • Detailed walkthroughs of TryHackMe and HackTheBox challenges
  • Deep dives into tools like Splunk, Wireshark, Suricata, and more
  • Study guides and preparation tips for CompTIA certifications
  • Python scripting for security automation
  • Log analysis techniques and best practices

My Current Focus

Right now, I'm focusing on:

  • CompTIA Security+: Preparing for the certification exam
  • Home Lab Expansion: Building out my test environment with new tools and scenarios
  • SIEM Mastery: Deepening my knowledge of Splunk for incident detection and response
  • Network Analysis: Advancing my skills in packet analysis and network forensics

Let's Connect

I believe that the best learning happens through collaboration and knowledge sharing. If you're also on a cybersecurity journey, whether you're just starting out or are an experienced professional, I'd love to connect and exchange insights.

Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn or through my contact page. I'm always open to discussing security topics, sharing resources, or collaborating on projects.

Stay Tuned

Thank you for taking the time to visit my blog. I'm committed to creating valuable, practical content that helps both beginners and intermediate practitioners in their cybersecurity journey. New posts will be published regularly, so be sure to check back often!

Whether you're here to learn, share, or simply explore, welcome aboard. Let's navigate the fascinating world of cybersecurity together.
